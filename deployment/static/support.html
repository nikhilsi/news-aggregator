Frequently Asked Questions

Where do the articles come from?

ClearNews aggregates articles from publicly available RSS feeds and financial news APIs — over 40 sources across 13 categories. We don't write or modify any content.

Why can't I read some articles in the reader view?

Some publishers restrict access to their content (paywalls, bot detection). When this happens, the app shows a "Read on [source]" button so you can visit the original site directly.

How often are articles updated?

Articles are refreshed automatically when you open the app. You can also pull down on the feed to force a fresh update from all sources.

Is an account required?

No. ClearNews works without any login or account. All features are available immediately.

Does the app work offline?

Previously loaded articles may be available from your device's cache, but an internet connection is needed to fetch new content.
