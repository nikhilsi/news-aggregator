ClearNews is a personal news aggregator that pulls from multiple sources and presents them through a clean, filterable interface. No accounts, no tracking, no ads.

ARTICLE FEED
Browse articles from 40+ curated sources across technology, business, science, world news, and finance. Pull-to-refresh with background cache warming delivers fresh content without waiting. Infinite scroll loads more articles as you read.

CATEGORY FILTERING
Filter articles by category — technology, business, science, world, finance, and more. Switch between categories with haptic-feedback pills.

SEARCH
Full-text search with 400ms debounce finds articles across all sources instantly.

READER VIEW
Read articles in a distraction-free reader view. Clean HTML with content security policy, dark mode CSS injection, and adjustable font size (S/M/L/XL). External links open in your browser.

SETTINGS
Choose between system, light, or dark themes. Adjust reader font size to your preference.

FEATURES
- 40+ curated news sources (RSS + financial APIs)
- Pull-to-refresh with background cache warming
- Category filtering and full-text search
- Clean reader view with dark mode and font scaling
- Infinite scroll with automatic pagination
- Share articles via Android share sheet
- Light, dark, and system theme support
- Haptic feedback on interactions
- No accounts, no tracking, no ads

Articles are aggregated from publicly available RSS feeds and financial news APIs. All content belongs to its respective publishers. Tap "Original" in the reader to visit the source.
